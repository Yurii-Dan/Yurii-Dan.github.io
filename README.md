## Projects

### Financial Data Analysis Project

This project analyzes financial data of a small business to identify revenue trends, cost drivers, and opportunities for optimization.

**Tools:** Python (Pandas), SQL (PostgreSQL), Tableau

#### Key Insights

- Top expense categories account for ~60–80% of total costs (Pareto principle)
- Salary and Marketing are the main cost drivers
- Clear seasonality in revenue and profit trends
- Business is highly dependent on a single revenue stream (~80% Sales)

#### Business Value

- Identified opportunities to optimize marketing expenses
- Highlighted risks related to revenue concentration
- Provided actionable insights for cost control and growth
